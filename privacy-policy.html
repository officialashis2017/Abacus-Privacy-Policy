1. Introduction

Welcome to Abacus Academy ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application for Computer Science & Application studies, featuring our innovative User ID-based ranking system.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: When you create an account or use our app, we collect your name, country, state, district, and other profile information.
  • Unique User ID: A timestamp-based unique identifier (USER_timestamp) is automatically generated for each user to ensure fair competition and prevent duplicate submissions.
  • Educational Data: We collect information about your exam preferences, subject selections, and learning progress.
  • Test Results: Your mock test scores, practice session results, and performance analytics.
  • Leaderboard Data: Your scores, rankings, and achievements for competitive features, identified by your unique User ID.
  • Feedback: Any feedback, comments, or reviews you provide about our app.

  • Usage Analytics: App usage patterns, features accessed, and interaction data.
  • Performance Data: App performance metrics, crash reports, and error logs.

  • Connectivity Data: Network status and connection quality for optimal app performance.
